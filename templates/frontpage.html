Protecting Bangladesh's Food Future

DigitalKrishi uses cutting-edge technology to prevent post-harvest losses, empower farmers, and ensure food security for millions across Bangladesh.

The Food Loss Crisis in Bangladesh

4.5 Million ৪.৫ মিলিয়ন Tonnes Lost

Bangladesh loses about 4.5 million metric tonnes of food grains annually due to inadequate storage facilities and poor handling practices.

$1.5 Billion $১.৫ বিলিয়ন Economic Loss

This represents an annual economic cost of approximately US $1.5 billion according to Bangladesh Institute of Development Studies.

12-32% ১২-৩২% of Staples Wasted

Approximately 12–32% of staple foods including rice, pulses, vegetables, meat, and dairy are lost annually during production and distribution.
